STEM Program

The Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM) strand prepares students for careers in engineering, medicine, computer science, and other STEM-related fields through advanced coursework and research opportunities.

Specialized Subjects:

  • General Biology, Chemistry, and Physics
  • Pre-Calculus and Basic Calculus
  • Earth Science
  • Disaster Readiness and Risk Reduction

ABM Program

The Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) strand focuses on business principles, management theories, and corporate operations, preparing students for careers in business administration, accounting, economics, and entrepreneurship.

Specialized Subjects:

  • Applied Economics
  • Business Math and Finance
  • Fundamentals of Accountancy, Business, and Management
  • Principles of Marketing
